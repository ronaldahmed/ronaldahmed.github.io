About me

I just graduated from Mechatronics Engineering (Robotics) at National University of Engineering (UNI) in Lima, Peru. Currently, I serve as vice-president of the Artificial Intelligence & Control Systems Research Laboratory (GISCIA) in the Mechanics Department at UNI. There, we conduct research in Robotics, Machine Learning and Finance, involving undergraduate students early in hands-on projects in these fields. We often organize seminars and lectures in order to attract and motivate the next generation of researchers in Peru.

Research Interests

I am interested in the application of Machine Learning to Natural Language Processsing tasks. I specially like semantics, machine translation, unsupervised learning, statistical inference and scalable machine learning.
