About me

I am a freshly graduated Mechatronics Engineer from the National University of Engineering in Lima - Peru. In addition, I am vice-president of the Artificial Intelligence Research Lab (GISCIA in Spanish), where I was advised by Alberto Coronado in research projects in Machine Learning.

I am passionate about pursuing further studies and research in the intersection of the fields of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I also enjoy doing web development and data visualization.

Research Interests

I am mainly interested in the application of statistical models to NLP tasks like syntactic parsing, semantics word sense disambiguation and machine translation. I have done some research in topic modelling and shallow parsing in the last year.
