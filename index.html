About me

I am a research assistant at the Dept. of EE and CS at Penn State, under the supervision of Professor Julio Urbina, working in classification of radar echoes data. Previously, I worked in the Research Institute of the National University of Engineering (UNI) (Lima, Peru), working in topic modeling and education.

In addition, I lead the Artificial Intelligence and Control Systems Research Lab (GISCIA, for its initial in Spanish), a lab part of the Mechanical Engineering Deparment at UNI.

My research interest spans the intersection of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing and Robotics, as well as to understand how humans acquire language at different stages of their lifes.

I graduated from Mechatronics Engineering (Robotics) at UNI. There, I worked with Prof. Alberto Coronado in Topic Modeling and bayesian inference.
