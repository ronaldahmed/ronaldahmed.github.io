About me

I just graduated from Mechatronics Engineering (Robotics) at National University of Engineering (UNI) in Lima, Peru. There, I was advised by Alberto Coronado in research projects in Machine Learning. Currently, I serve as vice-president of the Artificial Intelligence & Control Systems Research Laboratory (GISCIA) in the Mechanics Department at UNI. There, we conduct research in Robotics, Machine Learning, NLP, Computer vision and Finance, involving undergraduate students early in hands-on projects in these fields. We often organize seminars and lectures in order to attract and motivate the next generation of researchers in Peru.

Research Interests

I am mainly interested in the application of Machine Learning to NLP tasks like syntactic parsing, semantics, word sense disambiguation, topic modeling and machine translation.

Some interesting trends I have been following lately are deep learning, automatic image captioning, distributed/parallel machine learning, learning representations, active learning, among others.
